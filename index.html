Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our elevator services

Regular maintenance should be performed on a monthly basis for commercial elevators and quarterly for residential elevators. However, the frequency may vary depending on usage, age, and manufacturer recommendations. Our team can assess your specific needs and create a customized maintenance schedule.

With proper maintenance, a modern elevator can last 20-25 years before requiring major modernization. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can significantly extend the lifespan of your elevator system, ensuring safe and reliable operation for many years.

The installation timeline depends on various factors including elevator type, building structure, and complexity. On average, residential elevator installation takes 3-5 days, while commercial elevator installation can take 2-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Modern elevators come with numerous safety features including emergency brakes, overload sensors, door obstruction detection, emergency lighting, communication systems, and backup power. Our elevators exceed industry safety standards to provide peace of mind for building owners and passengers.
