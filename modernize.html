Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical return on investment for home modernization?
Most modernization projects provide 70-90% ROI, with kitchen and bathroom renovations, energy efficiency upgrades, and exterior improvements offering the highest returns. However, the exact ROI depends on your local market, the quality of work, and the specific upgrades chosen.
How long does a typical modernization project take?
Timeline varies based on scope. Minor modernization projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive renovations can require 3-6 months. During your consultation, we provide a detailed timeline specific to your project's requirements.
Can I live in my home during modernization?
It depends on the project scope. Minor updates allow continued occupancy, while major renovations might require temporary relocation. Our team works to minimize disruption and can phase work to accommodate your needs when possible.
Do I need permits for modernization projects?
Most significant renovations require permits, particularly those involving structural changes, electrical/plumbing modifications, or additions. Our team handles the permitting process, ensuring all work complies with local building codes and regulations.
How do you ensure quality in modernization projects?
We maintain quality through rigorous contractor vetting, regular site inspections, adherence to industry standards, and a comprehensive final verification process. All our work comes with warranties on both materials and workmanship.
