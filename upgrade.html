Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an elevator upgrade take?

Depending on the scale of the upgrade, most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Our team works efficiently to minimize downtime and disruption to your building's operations.

What are the cost benefits of upgrading?

Modern elevator systems typically reduce energy consumption by 30-70%, decrease maintenance costs by up to 40%, and can increase property values by improving building efficiency ratings and tenant satisfaction.

Can I upgrade specific components rather than the entire system?

Yes, we offer modular upgrades that allow you to improve specific components based on your priorities and budget. Common partial upgrades include control systems, door mechanisms, or cabin interiors.

Do upgraded elevators require special maintenance?

Modern elevator systems actually require less frequent maintenance due to improved reliability. We provide comprehensive maintenance programs with remote monitoring to ensure optimal performance.
